Our Story

Preserving India's rich cultural heritage through e-commerce

Our Mission

At Authentic India, we are passionate about showcasing the incredible artistry and craftsmanship of Indian artisans. Our mission is to connect these talented individuals with a global audience, providing them with fair compensation and recognition for their work.

Our Journey

Founded in 2020, Authentic India started as a small initiative to support local artisans during challenging times. Today, we have grown into a platform that represents hundreds of artisans from across India, bringing their unique creations to customers worldwide.

Our Values

Fair Trade

We ensure that our artisans receive fair compensation for their work.

Sustainability

We promote eco-friendly practices in production and packaging.

Authenticity

We guarantee the authenticity of each handcrafted product.

About Authentic India

Authentic India is an ecommerce platform dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural legacy of India. We work closely with local artisans and communities to bring you the finest handcrafted products that embody the essence of this remarkable country.
